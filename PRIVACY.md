# Persistent Pinned Tabs for Brave - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 1, 2026

## Summary

This browser extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** This extension does not:

- Collect personal information
- Transmit data over the internet
- Use analytics or tracking
- Communicate with any external servers

## Data Storage

All Pinns and app data are stored locally in your browser storage. Nothing leaves your device.

## Changes

We may update this policy occasionally, but will never snoop on you or collect analytics. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.
